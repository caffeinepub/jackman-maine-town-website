# Specification

## Summary
**Goal:** Add emergency services (Sheriff, Fire Department, Ambulance) with 911 phone links to the Town Services page.

**Planned changes:**
- Add a visually distinct "Emergency Services" section to the Town Services page with three entries: Sheriff, Fire Department, and Ambulance
- Each entry displays a clickable `tel:911` phone link that dials 911 on mobile devices
- Preserve and/or enhance the existing 911 emergency banner on the Services page
- Style the new section consistently with the deep forest green, slate gray, and warm wood-toned theme

**User-visible outcome:** Visitors to the Town Services page can clearly see and tap/click emergency contact links for the Sheriff, Fire Department, and Ambulance, all dialing 911.
