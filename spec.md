# Specification

## Summary
**Goal:** Build a public town website for Jackman, Maine (ZIP 04945) with informational sections, a local events system, and a contact form backed by a simple data store.

**Planned changes:**
- Homepage with hero image, "Jackman, Maine" branding, ZIP 04945, welcome message, and navigation
- "About Jackman" section with descriptive text covering location in Somerset County, Canadian border proximity, and outdoor highlights
- "Town Services" section listing Town Office contact info, public works, and emergency services
- "Local Events & News" section displaying events/announcements (title, date, description) stored in the backend, sorted by date
- "Recreation & Outdoors" section with four activity highlights (snowmobiling, fishing, hiking, Moose River) and recreation imagery
- "Contact Us" section with town address (ZIP 04945), placeholder phone number, and a contact form (name, email, message) that stores submissions in the backend and shows a confirmation message
- Backend actor storing events/announcements and contact form submissions
- Wilderness-themed visual design using deep forest greens, slate grays, and warm wood-toned accents with responsive layout

**User-visible outcome:** Visitors can browse information about Jackman, Maine, view upcoming events and news, explore recreation options, and submit contact messages through a fully themed, responsive town website.
